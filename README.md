# LT-W6

**Research and infrastructure arm of Loop Trade.**

We build in public — selectively. This organisation hosts the tools, language primitives, and research output that sit at the edge of Loop Trade's proprietary infrastructure. What lives here is real. It is also intentionally incomplete.

---

## What is Loop Trade?

Loop Trade is a cross-market arbitrage platform that allocates capital dynamically across commodities, surplus inventory, logistics, and cross-border trade systems across 40+ countries.

The core thesis: markets are fragmented, inefficiencies are structural, and the ability to identify and act on them faster than the market corrects creates compounding returns. Capital rotation is the engine.

Loop Trade is not a research project. It operates.

---

## What is LT-W6?

LT-W6 is the public layer. It exists for three reasons:

**To build in public with discipline.** The tools and language primitives we develop are shared here — not because we have to, but because open iteration makes them sharper. Community feedback is signal.

**To attract the right people.** Engineers, quants, logistics specialists, and domain experts who understand what they are looking at. If you read this and immediately see something missing or wrong, that reaction is exactly what we are looking for.

**To think out loud.** Research notes, market observations, and structural inefficiency theses that do not belong inside a proprietary system but are worth putting into the world.

## What is not here

The execution engine. The live data layer. The capital allocation logic. The proprietary tariff and freight resolution tables. The scoring models that run in production.

Those are Loop Trade's infrastructure. They are not open source and will not be.

What you see here connects to that infrastructure — but is not it.
